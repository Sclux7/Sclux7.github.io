Cimino excellently uses the three act structure to show the extent of the damage caused by the Vietnam War, showing it's ruinous effect on those at the time. The three act structure of the wedding, war and then return show how chaotic the war was, with the acts eventually blending into each other at the end showing the confusion caused by this war and how the various personalities of the war have created a devastating culture. The motif of the "one shot" persists throughout the film, with the motifs presence being especially relevant in the second act of the film in Vietnam as not just the game of Russian Roulette that completely strips the plaeyrs of identity, leaving only the winner and the loser, but exists also in soldiers on both sides chances of survival. Cimino likens this war to the looming chance of that one in six bullet, placing a very real fear directly into the hands of those seeking an opportunity from the war, or an escape from the grim reality that surrounds them, and strips soldiers on both sides of identity, instead making everyone either the winner or the loser, devoid of hope, everything up to chance.

Why does this website exist?

A lot of you in fact no one ever has asked me why I made this website

I made this website earlier this year for a number of reasons. I've been adding things here and there for a couple months but I think it's important to write here why I do any of this

The most important reason is that the internet is becoming a more and more limiting place. It feels now that everyone is being forced into smaller and smaller boxes, the only form of self expression can be through someone else's interface, some big company's design rather than your own. It doesn't have to be this way, and that starts with you!

Don't let a big company tell you how to express yourself online. Make a place for yourself on the internet and make it exactly how you want it.

The website neocities is a great place to start! It's free, provides an easy way to view other people's websites, and is a great way to learn how to make a website.

Of course, learning how to code in html, css, javascript, etc might seem like an arduous task at first, but fear not! There are an endless amount of guides online that will help you through making your own website, and the reward of seeing the place you built at the end of it makes it all worth it.

To help you, my dear reader, get started, I will impart on to you three tips that helped me make this place the way I wanted it

Inspect element is your best friend ever for real

See something somewhere that you want to make? Steal it! Learn how it works! Open inspect element (ctrl shift c) and hover over whatever it is and see how it works!

Google it

Simple yes but like seriously. Just add html to the end and undoubtedly someone has a guide on how to do it

Just go for it!

You've got this and I believe in you :3
